What is the Mentor Program?

The eXp Realty India Mentor Program provides new agents with a dedicated partner and a clear path to gain the essential knowledge required to launch and build a successful real estate career. Each new agent, known as a Mentee, is paired with an experienced, certified agent who is approved by eXp Realty India and eXp Global. The Country Leader Team carefully matches Mentees with Mentors from their geographic region.

Certified Mentors are proficient in the real estate field and approved by the Country Leader Team. Their objective is to offer guidance, practical advice, and hands-on support to new eXp agents, leading them through each step of their first required transactions.

Mentees also benefit from a foundational curriculum through eXp’s learning management system, Trakstar, covering a wide range of real estate fundamentals.

Why Mentorship is Important?

Mentorship provides invaluable guidance and support, helping individuals navigate their career paths and personal development. It fosters skill enhancement, confidence, and builds strong professional networks, setting the stage for future success.

Who Can Join the Mentor Program?

The Mentor Program is available to any agent who is new or seeking additional support to grow their real estate business.

How Can I Join the Mentor Program?

To join the Mentor Program, you must be a part of eXp Realty India. Interested? Register here. Once your onboarding is complete, we will pair you with a Mentor based on your location. For more information, contact us at info@expglobalindia.com.
